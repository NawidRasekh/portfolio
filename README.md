## Projects

### [ev-road-pricing-denmark](./ev-road-pricing-denmark/)
**Road Pricing in Denmark's Electric Vehicle Transition: A Welfare Analysis of VKT Externalities**

Simulates five road-pricing policy scenarios as Denmark's car fleet electrifies (2026–2035).
Builds a driver-level welfare model with heterogeneous price elasticities (income, age, location)
to decompose the effects on consumer surplus, government revenue, congestion externalities, and
distributional equity across income groups.

`Python` · `pandas` · `NumPy` · `matplotlib` · `welfare economics` · `Pigouvian taxation`

### [programming-for-economists](./programming-for-economists/)
**Computational Methods in Applied Macroeconomics and Microeconomics**

Three projects combining formal economic theory with production-quality Python implementation:
structural inflation analysis (CPI vs. HICP decomposition via live APIs), labour-supply modelling
under nonlinear taxation (four-step FOC solver for kinked budget constraints, Gini/Lorenz welfare
analysis), and computational general equilibrium (Walrasian equilibrium with CES preferences,
tâtonnement vs. Newton-Raphson, AS-AD macro simulation).

`Python` · `pandas` · `SciPy` · `Danmarks Statistik API` · `FRED` · `constrained optimisation` · `general equilibrium`
